Brooklyn Moon Cafe

745 Fulton St, Brooklyn, NY 11217


This is a neighborhood spot that has so much culture packed in it, friendly atmosphere, and cheap happy hours. If you are looking for a Sunday brunch, this is your spot with only $1 mimosas! Great place to come for a date night and they even have poetry/spoken word that you can come check out! Check out the yelp reviews here.


Sweet Science

This is a bar-restaurant that you can hang out on a Saturday night and come back Sunday morning for brunch. It serves American bar food with a twist from tempura pickles to cheeseburger with maple-glazed pineapple, everything on this menu is mouthwatering! Check out the yelp reviews here.
