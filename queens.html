Vibes

143-06 Liberty Ave, Jamaica, NY 11435


In Queens and looking for a great soul food? Vibes is your place! They are known for their amazing red velvet waffles and chicken that will melt in your mouth! This is a great place to check out if you are looking for that southern cooking. Check out the yelp reviews here.

Pa-Nash NYC

144-14 243rd St, Rosedale, NY 11422


This is an elegant seating restaurant, great for grabbing cocktails or for a dinner date. This place is known for its Eurosoul fusion, which combines Mediterranean, Caribbean and soul food options. If you are looking to try something new, this is your place. Check out the yelp reviews here.


The Door

Rosedale, NY 163-07 Baisley Blvd, Jamaica, NY 11434

This place has live reggae music on Saturdays and all you can eat brunch on Sundays, it is definitely a restaurant you must come see if you are in the area. Also serves affordable Jamaican food seven days a week! They serve a great goat that is covered in a mustard-heavy curry paste, delicious! Check out the yelp reviews here.


Henrica Restaurant and Lounge

Rosedale, NY 138-89 Francis Lewis Blvd, Rosedale, NY 11422

This restaurant/lounge has a great laid back atmosphere surrounded by Jamaica vibrations. On Friday nights you can dance to the live Jamaican band playing or come try out the Jamaican dishes with a Chinese fusion. They have codfish fritters to sweet and sour jerk chicken, this place has great unique dishes! Check out the yelp reviews here.
